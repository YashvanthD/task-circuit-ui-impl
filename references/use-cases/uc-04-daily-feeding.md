## Business Rules

1. **No count change** - Feeding does NOT affect fish count
2. **Stock must be active** - Cannot feed terminated stock
3. **Quantity positive** - Must be > 0
4. **Date validation** - Cannot record future feedings
5. **FCR calculation** - Feed total used for FCR calculation

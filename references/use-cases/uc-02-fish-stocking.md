## Business Rules

1. **One active stock per pond** - A pond can only have one active stock at a time
2. **Pond must be empty** - Cannot stock fish in a pond with existing stock
3. **Species must exist** - Species must be in the catalog
4. **Count must be positive** - Cannot stock 0 or negative fish
5. **Auto-calculate biomass** - `total_biomass_kg = count Ã— avg_weight_g / 1000`
